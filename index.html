About Us

Nunawading Birds & Pets is your one stop shop for all your bird and pet needs. We are the oldest running bird business in Australia, having been in business since 1956. We specialise in a huge range of pet birds, aviary birds, rabbits and guinea pigs. We also have a great range of pet supplies including food, products and accessories, bulk seed and speciality mixes.

  • Pet Birds

    Unlike aviary birds, pet birds are usually not in the cage all the time as they love human contact. Talk to us and we will be able to assist you in selecting "the special bird" that suits your personality.

  • Aviary Birds

    We only sell aviary bred and hand reared cockatoos and galahs. Please be very wary when purchasing baby cockies and galahs. No trapped birds sold. Please note that due to climate differences between states we do not import lifestock.
